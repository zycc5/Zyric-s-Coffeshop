    Our Story

    Zyrine & Cedric founded Zyric on a simple belief: that coffee is more than a drink; it is a moment of stillness in a loud world. What started as a shared passion for the perfect roast has evolved into a sanctuary for the local community.

    Our philosophy is "Less, but better." By sourcing single-origin beans and roasting them in small batches, we ensure that every extraction honors the hands that grew the coffee.
